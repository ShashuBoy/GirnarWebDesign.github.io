Music Club

  • Home
  • Music Club

Known for uniting the music fraternity of IIT Delhi, the music club promotes the music culture among the students and faculty alike, giving a stage to willing performers and a platform to beginners to cultivate their skills. It organizes an array of events encompassing many genres, both Western and Eastern.Events like 'Mehfil' combine ghazals, qawwalis and Indian folk into a seamless and unforgettable experience.

As it is said,"The journey of a thousand miles begins with one step". Well here at IIT, our hostel music club provides you that "first step" in this field. The music culture of Girnar has always been one of the best, winning various competitions at the institute level. Weekly workshops are held to help you find and bring out the best in you, whatever it maybe, being good at singing or having a good hand with the instruments. Just be regular at the workshops and it will definitely help you explore the endless world of music.
