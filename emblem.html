About Emblem

  • Home
  • About Emblem

The residents of Girnar are popularly called the Lions in the Wild for Girnar Lions have always been the top contenders whenever people consider hostels for lifting trophies and cups, and this is what our Emblem stands for. With a ferocious lion roaring and preparing itself to pounce upon its enemies, none of the residents doubt that the cub has indeed grown. The lions always look forward to roaring their way out every season.
