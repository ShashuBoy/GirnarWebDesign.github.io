Dance Club

  • Home
  • Dance Club

The Dance Club is one of the most glamorous club of IIT Delhi. We organize events which fill the entire institute with enthusiasm and energy. After BRCA orientation, Duo is the first inter-hostel event which pulls a humongous crowd. Cheering in the events clearly shows the energy of the dancers and audience. Events like Group Dance, Fresher's Video and IDP(Institute Dance Production) give the freshers a chance to show off their talent and learn from the finest dancers of the institute.

Girnar has produced a lot of talented dancers in a very few years. With the passion of Dance in their hearts, residents of Girnar represent their hostel and their college. The hostel has dancers in both Choreo and Western Dance Societies of IIT Delhi. They perform in Rendezvous and many other college fests with full energy and grace. Like every BRCA club, Dance Club is a very essential part of the hostel culture. If you have passion and enthusiasm, we will teach you how to be a part of something big, something better.
