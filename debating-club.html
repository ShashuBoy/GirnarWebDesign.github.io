Debating Club

  • Home
  • Debating Club

One of the most respected societies in IIT Delhi, the Debating Club has a long standing tradition of excellence. With various events like Extempore, Parliamentary Debates and MUNs, this club presents a plethora of opportunities for students. On the way, students get to polish their communication skills, and gain self-confidence.

Girnar has had a great tradition winning debating championships in IIT Delhi and also spreading our wings outside the institute. With the most reputed debaters of the institute graduating this year from Girnar, it’s your turn to continue the legacy, as the debating club welcomes you into this world of amazing possibilities.
